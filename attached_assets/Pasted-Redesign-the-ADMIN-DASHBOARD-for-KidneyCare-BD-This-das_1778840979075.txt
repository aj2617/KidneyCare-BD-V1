Redesign the ADMIN DASHBOARD for "KidneyCare BD". This dashboard is used by public health officers and policymakers. It currently features a CKD heatmap, report generator, user management, policy simulator, and cohort tracking. Make it a powerful, modern analytics hub that works beautifully on both large desktop screens and mobile devices.

KEY REQUIREMENTS:
1. **Dashboard Overview**  
   - KPI cards at top: Total Patients, High Risk %, Average GFR, Districts Covered.  
   - Mini trend lines (last 30 days) inside each KPI card.

2. **Interactive Heatmap (Bangladesh)**  
   - Full‑width Leaflet.js map with district GeoJSON.  
   - Layer toggles: Patient density, Risk level, Gender, Age group, Urban/Rural.  
   - On mobile, map occupies top half; drill‑down by tapping a district shows summary stats.

3. **Report & Simulator Hub**  
   - “Generate Report” wizard: select type (National burden, Gender, Age, Stage, GFR comparison), date range, format (PDF/Excel).  
   - Budget Impact Simulator with sliders for population, intervention cost; live projected savings chart.  
   - Recent downloads section.

4. **Cohort & Research Tools**  
   - Cohort creator: select criteria (age, stage, district) → track outcomes over time.  
   - Anonymized export (CSV) for approved researchers.  
   - Annual report auto‑generation button.

5. **User & CHW Management**  
   - Searchable table of all users with role badges, approval status.  
   - Quick approve/disable toggles.  
   - Map view of CHW assignments (dots on map).

6. **Design Language**  
   - Professional, data‑driven aesthetic: white cards, bold numbers, ample spacing.  
   - Use the same brand colors for consistency.  
   - Responsive: KPI grid collapses from 4 cols to 2 to 1 on mobile.  
   - Charts and maps should be touch‑enabled (pan, pinch‑zoom).

7. **PWA & Performance**  
   - Map tiles may be heavy; use lazy loading and cached tiles via service worker.  
   - Reports generation happens server‑side; show download progress.

8. **Language & Accessibility**  
   - Bilingual UI.  
   - All tables and filters must work with screen readers and keyboard navigation.  

Deliver an admin dashboard that turns raw patient data into actionable policy insights, equally usable on a desktop monitor in Dhaka and on a tablet in a rural field office.
