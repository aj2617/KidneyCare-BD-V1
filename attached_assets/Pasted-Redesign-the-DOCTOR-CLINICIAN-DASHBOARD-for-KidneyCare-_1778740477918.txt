Redesign the DOCTOR / CLINICIAN DASHBOARD for "KidneyCare BD". The current dashboard includes patient list, patient profile with timeline, alerts, GFR comparison, and prescription tools. Transform it into a modern, efficient clinical workspace optimized for both mobile rounds and desktop analysis.

KEY REQUIREMENTS:
1. **Adaptive Layout**  
   - Desktop: sidebar with navigation (Patients, Alerts, Teleconsult, Prescriptions).  
   - Mobile: bottom navigation with hamburger menu for extra options.  
   - Always responsive without horizontal scroll.

2. **Patient List (Home)**  
   - Searchable/sortable list or grid cards showing patient avatar, name, risk badge (colored), latest GFR, and last vitals date.  
   - Quick filters: “All”, “Critical”, “Stage 3+”, “Unlogged >7 days”.  
   - Infinite scroll or virtualized list for large patient panels.

3. **Alerts Panel**  
   - Notification bell with unread count badge.  
   - Cards grouped by severity (Critical, Warning, Reminder).  
   - Tap an alert → deep‑links directly to that patient’s relevant chart/record.  
   - “Mark all as read” and “Snooze” actions.

4. **Patient Detail Page (Timeline)**  
   - Unified timeline combining vitals (BP, creatinine, glucose) in interactive Recharts graphs.  
   - Milestone markers (stage changes, referrals) as pins on the timeline.  
   - Side‑by‑side GFR comparison (MDRD, CG, CKD‑EPI) with color‑coded staging.  
   - Quick actions: “Log Vitals”, “Issue Prescription”, “Start Teleconsult”.

5. **Telemedicine Integration**  
   - “Call Patient” button launches WebRTC video call in a floating window.  
   - During call, the doctor can view the patient’s latest GFR chart overlaid (picture‑in‑picture).

6. **E‑Prescription**  
   - Structured form: medicine name, dosage, frequency, duration.  
   - Live preview of Bengali prescription with QR code.  
   - Print/share as PDF.

7. **Modern Design Elements**  
   - Card‑based layout with subtle elevation.  
   - Use the color system: danger for critical values, warning for borderline.  
   - Skeleton loading for data fetches.  
   - Mobile‑optimized data tables (collapsible rows, horizontal scroll only for tables).

8. **PWA & Offline**  
   - Cached patient list and recent records for offline review.  
   - Alerts sync when back online; background sync for any offline actions.

9. **Language & Accessibility**  
   - Bilingual (EN/BN) toggle, clearly visible.  
   - Large touch targets, high contrast for clinical use.  

Deliver a complete redesign that makes a busy Bangladeshi doctor feel in control, with all critical information no more than two taps away.
